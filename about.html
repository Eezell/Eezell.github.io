About Neighborly AI

We believe everyone deserves a patient, friendly guide to the world of AI — no tech degree required.

Meet Ezell

Hey there! I'm Ezell, the founder of Neighborly AI. I started this because I kept seeing the same thing — smart, capable people feeling left out of the AI conversation, just because nobody took the time to explain it in plain English.

I'm not here to impress you with fancy tech talk. I'm here to sit down with you, show you how these tools actually work, and help you use them for your real life — whether that's writing better emails, organizing your schedule, or giving your small business a boost.

I'm based in Des Moines, Iowa, but I work with folks everywhere. Think of me as that neighbor who's always happy to help — except instead of fixing your fence, I'm showing you how to get the most out of AI.
